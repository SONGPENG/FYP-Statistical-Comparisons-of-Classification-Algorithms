About us

Welcome To Peng's Graduation Projrct - Hypothesis Testing for Classification Tool

Thanks for using this visualization tool. I am a computer science student from Beijing Jiaotong University and Lancaster University. This tool is my graduation project with the programming in Python framework, and it will be continuously updated in the future.

My effort mainly includes the above four parts - Hypothesis Testing, General Evaluation Models, Data Dimensionality Reduction and kNN classifier.

What we provide

Hypothesis Testing

Help you find out significant differences among classifiers over single or multiple test dataset(s) through 8 statistical strategies.

Evaluation Models

General evaluation models are applied for visualization. Including overall performance, ROC diagram, critical distance, Texas sharpshooter.

Dimensionality Reduction

We have 7 different ways for the data dimensionality reduction, and also provide visualization tools. For examples: PCA, t-SNE, LDA, Auto Encoder.

kNN & Time Series

If you don't have classifiers and datasets, use kNN and time series we provided!
